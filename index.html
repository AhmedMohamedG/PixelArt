Instructions

  • Choose your canvas height, width and cell size.
  • pick a color, you can change it later.
  • draw by a single click or drag your mouse while clicking.
  • erase one cell by double click on it or use erase button to erase all cells.
  • use random fill to fill empty cells with random colors and reclick to stop.
  • Caution: Do not under any circumstances use random fill and hide borders together, you will probably get dizzy or worse.

Enjoy !!
